About Aurora Hall

A boutique venue with a rich history of unforgettable moments.

Our History

Aurora Hall opened in 1998 as a restored landmark theater. Over the years we have hosted thousands of events — from intimate ceremonies to city-wide festivals.

Mission

To provide an elevated, stress-free event experience where every detail is handled with care and creativity.
